About Us

Joe Bushnell, our Dad, is a Louisiana born transplant to Seattle, WA, where he has made his home since 1959. Dad developed the carefully selected blend of seasonings in 1983. Broussard's Creole Foods offers a variety of gourmet seasoning solutions for restaurants, specialty stores, state & federal government cafeterias, hotels, cruise ships, and business enterprises.

When Dad isn't promoting Broussard's in the marketplace, he spends his time cooking and develops recipes for his recipe booklets. Check out the recipe booklet. for traditional Creole or Cajun favorites such as gumbo, boudin, and etouffee. Whether you're preparing meat, vegetables, or seafood, you can add the wonderful flavor of Broussard's Creole Seasoning. The original recipe was passed to Dad from his father who used it to cure meat in southern Louisiana. Marinating ham and sausage with the spices and herbs before smoking gives them an incredible taste. Beef, pork, and chicken dishes, gumbo, catfish, french fries, sauces stews, and soups are among the many dishes enhanced by Broussard's Creole Seasoning.

About Ike Durham

Ike Durham has 8 siblings, 6 who served in the military and 2 who graduated from college, himself and his sister. After graduating from college in 1981, he worked as an investment broker at Payne and Webber Broker for the next 30 plus years, retiring in 2014. After retiring, Ike bought Broussard's Creole Foods from his wife's family in 2011. Ike currently resides in Kent, WA with his wife.
