Roux

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oil or shortening
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of Broussards Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning

Heat oil or shortening in a heavy skillet. Add flour and stir continuously until dark brown. Add Broussards Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning for flavor. After reaching desired color, remove from skillet to stop cooking process.
