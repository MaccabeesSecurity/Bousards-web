Roux

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oil or shortening
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of Broussards Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat oil or shortening in a heavy skillet.
  2. Add flour and stir continuously until dark brown.
  3. Add Broussards Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning for flavor.
  4. After reaching desired color, remove from skillet to stop cooking process.

New Orleans Gumbo

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe roux (see Roux recipe above)
  • 2 qt water
  • 3 bunches green onions (chopped)
  • 1 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 whole bodied crab (cleaned and legs separated)
  • 1 lb. prawns
  • 1 lb. shelled shrimp
  • 1 fryer chicken (cut up)
  • 1 lb. smoked Louisiana hot link sausages
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Add roux to boiling water
  2. After boiling resumes, add vegetables.
  3. Boil slowly for 15 minutes.
  4. Add rest of ingredients.
  5. Simmer for about 45 minutes or until chicken is done

Serve in bowls over cooked rice. Serves 6.

Shrimp / Crawfish Etoufee

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb. peeled crayfish tails or shrimp
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 whole onion (chopped)
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped fine)
  • 2 garlic cloves (chopped fine)
  • 2 tbs. flour
  • 1 tbs. paprika
  • 1/4 cup green onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbs. parsley (chopped)
  • 2 cups water
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Season crayfish with Broussards' seasoning - set aside.
  2. Heat margarine in pot.
  3. Saute crayfish until firm.
  4. Remove crayfish from pot.
  5. Place all vegetables in pot and saute 10 minutes.
  6. Return crayfish to pot, add water.
  7. Simmer 35 - 45 minutes.
  8. Add more seasoning to taste.
  9. Completely dissolve flour in small amount of water.
  10. Add to pot to thicken.
  11. Allow to simmer 5 more minutes.

Serve in bowls over cooked rice. Serves 5.

Note: Do not use iron pots and pans with crayfish. It will discolor the crayfish.

Fried Sweet Potatoes

Cooking Instructions

  1. Peel and cut sweet potatoes as you would for french fries.
  2. Deep fry until brown.
  3. Drain.
  4. While still warm, sprinkle with brown or white sugar.

Serve with pork chops or ham. If served with beef, sprinkle Broussard's seasoning over potatoes.

Broussard's Chicken Stew

Ingredients

  • 1/2 roux recipe (see roux recipe)
  • 1 stewing chicken (about 5 lbs. cut up)
  • 1 green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 onion (chopped fine)
  • 1 bunch green onions (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley (chopped)
  • 1 tbs. paprika
  • 2 white potatoes (quartered)
  • 2 turnips (quartered)
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil hen in water until tender.
  2. Reduce stock to 2 quarts.
  3. Add remaining ingredients.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes, until potatoes are tender.
  5. Season for taste

Deep Fried Okra

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. fresh whole okra
  • 1 cup corn meal
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning

Cooking Instructions

  1. Wash okra.
  2. Slice in 1/2 inch round pieces.
  3. Season well.
  4. Pour corn meal into a brown paper bag.
  5. Add okra and shake well to coat.
  6. Slowly add and fry coated okra in deep fat until brown.
  7. Remove with slotted spoon and drain

Serve as an appetizer or as a vegetable side dish.

Eggplant, Shrimp, & Crab Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup celery (chopped)
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup parsley (minced)
  • 1/2 cup saltine crackers (crushed)
  • 1/4 tsp Louisiana red hot sauce
  • 1 lb. cleaned shrimp
  • 1 lb. crab meat
  • 1/2 lb. parmesan cheese
  • 4 strips bacon
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • Juice from 1/2 lemon
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • Dash of Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning

Cooking Instructions

  1. Peel and slice eggplant.
  2. Soak in Broussard's seasoned water for 1 hour.
  3. Fry bacon until crisp and remove from skillet.
  4. Add onions, celery, bell pepper, and garlic to skillet.
  5. Fry vegetables for 5 minutes.
  6. Add remaining ingredients.
  7. Mix well and pour into a buttered casserole dish.
  8. Top with cheese and bread crumbs.
  9. Bake 40 minutes at 350 degrees.

Creole Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice (day old rice is best)
  • 1/4 lb. chopped ham
  • 1/4 cup green onion (chopped)
  • Dash of Louisiana red hot sauce
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Lightly fry ham, onion, and pepper in margarine until onion is tender.
  2. Add rice to skillet.
  3. Heat until warm all through.
  4. Beat eggs.
  5. Pour over rice and stir until well mixed.
  6. Season to taste.
  7. Add hot sauce.
  8. Continue heating 3 - 4 minutes.

Okra & Corn Etoufee

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. fresh or frozen okra
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1 lb. frozen corn
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 tbs oil
  • 1 green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 fresh tomatoes (chopped)
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Wash and cut okra into 1/2 inch round slices.
  2. Fry okra in hot oil until stickiness is gone.
  3. Add onion, bell pepper, and corn.
  4. Continue to fry until onion is tender.
  5. Add tomato and continue frying for 3 - 4 more minutes.
  6. Add water and season to taste.
  7. Simmer 5 minutes.

The etoufee may be served over cooked rice or as a side dish.

Note: Don't cook okra in iron pot. The okra can be discolored.

Baton Rouge Pralines

Ingredients

  • 1 cup seasme seeds
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup white kano syrup
  • 1 tsp. vanilla
  • 1/2 stick margarine
  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1/2 cup pecan halves

Cooking Instructions

  1. Roast sesame seeds in heavy skillet until lightly brown.
  2. Set aside.
  3. Mix remaining ingredients in a heavy saucepan.
  4. Stir over medium heat to a full boil until sugar dissolves.
  5. Add seeds and stir.
  6. Pour 3 inch circles of mixture onto a well greased cookie sheet.
  7. Let cool.

Roast Grouse

Ingredients

  • 4 grouse
  • 1/2 cup celery (chopped)
  • 1 tbs. butter
  • 4 slices bacon
  • 2 tbs. onion (chopped)
  • 1 tsp. Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning

Cooking Instructions

  1. Sprinkle grouse inside and outside with Broussard's seasoning.
  2. Set aside.
  3. Lightly saute celery and onion in butter.
  4. Add 1 tsp. seasoning.
  5. Stuff grouse with vegetables.
  6. Cover each grouse breast with a slice of bacon.
  7. Place in a shallow roasting pan.
  8. Bake for 30 minutes at 350 degrees.

Roast Pheasant with Wine

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 slices bacon
  • 4 pheasants
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 2 medium onions (sliced)
  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • 1 tsp. Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning

Cooking Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a dutch oven.
  2. Season pheasant inside and outside.
  3. Brown in butter until lightly brown all over.
  4. Add wine, water, and onions.
  5. Bake uncovered at 375 degrees for 1 hour.
  6. Baste often.

Baked Rabbit

Ingredients

  • 1 large rabbit (cut into pieces)
  • Flour to coat rabbit
  • 1 large onion (diced)
  • 2 tbs. Worchestershire sauce
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup celery (sliced)
  • 1 cup water

Cooking Instructions

  1. Wash rabbit in cold water.
  2. Pat dry.
  3. Sprinkle rabbit with Broussard's seasoning.
  4. Dip each piece in flour.
  5. Fry in dutch oven in hot oil until brown on all sides.
  6. Add rest of ingredients.
  7. Season to taste with additional Broussards's seasoning.
  8. Cover and bake 2 hours at 350 degrees.

Roast Venison

Ingredients

  • 1 venison roast (3 - 5 lbs.)
  • Flour to coat
  • 1 green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 beer
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/2 cup onion (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup parsley
  • 3 strips bacon

Cooking Instructions

  1. Season roast with Broussard's seasoning.
  2. Coat with flour.
  3. Melt margarine in heavy dutch oven.
  4. Brown roast on all sides.
  5. Add rest of ingredients.
  6. Lay bacon over roast.
  7. Cover and bake at 350 degrees for 2 1/2 hours or until tender.
  8. Remove meat from juices.
  9. Add small amount of thickener (flour or corn starch).

Serve over roast slices.

Frog Legs

Ingredients

  • 8 frog legs
  • Flour to coat
  • 1 green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup parsley (finely chopped)
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • 1 lb. can tomatoes
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Season legs.
  2. Coat with flour.
  3. Brown in oil over medium heat.
  4. Cook until done.

Shrimp Creole

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup margarine (1/2 stick)
  • 2 medium carrots (coarsely chopped)
  • 1 cut celery (chopped)
  • 1 green pepper (chopped)
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 16 oz. can tomato sauce
  • 3/4 cup water
  • 1/4 cup parsley (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp. Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning
  • 1 1/2 lbs. raw shrimp (shelled and cleaned)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Saute vegetables in margarine until tender.
  2. Add tomato sauce, water, parsley, and seasoning.
  3. Simmer 15 minutes.
  4. Turn heat up to medium.
  5. Add shrimp and allow to heat through.
  6. Simmer 10 minutes.

Serve over cooked rice.

Blackened Seafood, Meats, & Poultry

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat a dry cast iron skillet on high until very hot.
  2. In about 10 minutes, the skillet should begin to turn white.
  3. Brush meat that is about 1/2 inch thick with butter.
  4. Season both sides with Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning.
  5. Cook each side until outside is black (2 - 3 minutes).

For seafood and poultry, try Broussard's Lemon Seafood Seasoning

Catfish Etouffe

Ingredients

  • 4 tbs. roux (see roux recipe)
  • 1 cup onions (chopped fine)
  • 1 cup green onion (chopped fine and including green tops)
  • 1/2 cup celery (fine chopped)
  • 1/2 cup green pepper (fine chopped)
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper (chopped)
  • 3 cups water
  • 1 16 oz. can tomatoes (drained and coarsely chopped)
  • 1 tsp. lemon juice
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)
  • 2 lbs. catfish (cleaned, skinned, and cut into steak pieces)
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley (fine chopped)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Warm roux in a large casserole over low heat stirring constantly.
  2. Add onions, green onions, celery, green pepper, and garlic.
  3. Stir constantly.
  4. Cook for 5 minutes, or until soft and tender, but not brown.
  5. Add water, tomatoes, and lemon juice.
  6. Use Broussard's seasoning to taste.
  7. Cook on low for 30 minutes lightly covered.
  8. Add catfish (or other fish, shellfish).
  9. Add parsley, and stir gently to mix fish with the sauce.
  10. Cover partially
  11. Cook for 10 minutes without stirring until fish is done (flakes).

Serve over cooked rice or mashed potatoes Serves 4.

Crawfish or Shrimp / Ham Jambolaya

Ingredients

  • 2 cups water
  • 2 tbs. Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning
  • 2 lbs. uncooked shrimp (shelled and cleaned)
  • 6 tbs. butter
  • 1 1/2 cups onion (fine chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (fine chopped)
  • 1 16 oz. can tomatoes (drained and chopped fine, keep juice)
  • 1/4 cup celery (fine chopped)
  • 1/4 cup green pepper (fine chopped)
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley (fine chopped)
  • 1/2 tsp. cloves
  • 1/2 tsp. thyme
  • 1 cup long grain white rice
  • 1 lb. cooked lean smoked ham (cut in diced cubes)
  • 4 tbs. tomato paste

Cooking Instructions

  1. Drop shrimp into boiling seasoned water.
  2. Cover completely.
  3. Cook briskly uncovered for 5 minutes, or until pink and firm.
  4. In large casserole dish, melt butter over moderate heat.
  5. When foam begins to subside, add onion and garlic, stirring frequently.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes, or until soft and tender.
  7. Add tomatoes, tomato juice, and tomato paste.
  8. Stir over medium heat for 5 minutes.
  9. Add celery, green pepper, parsley, cloves, and thyme.
  10. Season with Broussard's seasoning to taste.
  11. Stir frequently.
  12. Cook uncovered over medium heat until vegetables are tender and mixture is thick.
  13. Add the ham, cook for 5 minutes.
  14. Add the shrimp, cook for 5 more minutes.
  15. Add the rice.
  16. Stir over medium heat until mixture is hot and rice has absorbed any liquid in the pan.

Season to taste. Serves 6 to 8.

Rice Dressing

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. chicken hearts
  • 1/2 lb. chicken livers
  • 1 green pepper (chopped)
  • 3 tbs. cooking oil
  • 2 cups cooked long grain white rice
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley (fine chopped)
  • 1/2 lb. chicken gizzards
  • 1 cup green onion (chopped)
  • 1/3 celery (chopped)
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil chicken parts in enough water to cover until tender.
  2. Reserve liquid.
  3. Put cooked chicken parts and vegetables (except parsley) through fine blade of a food grinder.
  4. In a heavy casserole dish, heat cooking oil over moderate heat.
  5. Add the ground chicken mixture.
  6. Stir in Broussard's seasoning to taste.
  7. Reduce heat to low.
  8. Cook uncovered until vegetables are tender and chicken mixture is richly brown.
  9. Stir occasionally.
  10. When chicken mixture is done, fluff rice with fork and add to casserole.
  11. With fork, toss rice and chicken mixture together gently, but thoroughly.
  12. You may want to add some of the reserved liquid to make dish more moist.
  13. Taste for seasoning and stir in parsley.

Serve at once. Serves 6 to 8.
