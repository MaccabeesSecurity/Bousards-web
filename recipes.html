Roux

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of oil or shortening
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of Broussards Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning

Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat oil or shortening in a heavy skillet.
  2. Add flour and stir continuously until dark brown.
  3. Add Broussards Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning for flavor.
  4. After reaching desired color, remove from skillet to stop cooking process.

New Orleans Gumbo

Ingredients

  • 1 recipe roux (see Roux recipe above)
  • 2 qt water
  • 3 bunches green onions (chopped)
  • 1 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 whole bodied crab (cleaned and legs separated)
  • 1 lb. prawns
  • 1 lb. shelled shrimp
  • 1 fryer chicken (cut up)
  • 1 lb. smoked Louisiana hot link sausages
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Add roux to boiling water
  2. After boiling resumes, add vegetables.
  3. Boil slowly for 15 minutes.
  4. Add rest of ingredients.
  5. Simmer for about 45 minutes or until chicken is done

Serve in bowls over cooked rice. Serves 6.

Shrimp / Crawfish Etoufee

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 lb. peeled crayfish tails or shrimp
  • 1/2 cup butter or margarine
  • 1 whole onion (chopped)
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped fine)
  • 2 garlic cloves (chopped fine)
  • 2 tbs. flour
  • 1 tbs. paprika
  • 1/4 cup green onion (chopped)
  • 2 tbs. parsley (chopped)
  • 2 cups water
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Season crayfish with Broussards' seasoning - set aside.
  2. Heat margarine in pot.
  3. Saute crayfish until firm.
  4. Remove crayfish from pot.
  5. Place all vegetables in pot and saute 10 minutes.
  6. Return crayfish to pot, add water.
  7. Simmer 35 - 45 minutes.
  8. Add more seasoning to taste.
  9. Completely dissolve flour in small amount of water.
  10. Add to pot to thicken.
  11. Allow to simmer 5 more minutes.

Serve in bowls over cooked rice. Serves 5.

Note: Do not use iron pots and pans with crayfish. It will discolor the crayfish.

Fried Sweet Potatoes

Cooking Instructions

  1. Peel and cut sweet potatoes as you would for french fries.
  2. Deep fry until brown.
  3. Drain.
  4. While still warm, sprinkle with brown or white sugar.

Serve with pork chops or ham. If served with beef, sprinkle Broussard's seasoning over potatoes.

Broussard's Chicken Stew

Ingredients

  • 1/2 roux recipe (see roux recipe)
  • 1 stewing chicken (about 5 lbs. cut up)
  • 1 green bell pepper (chopped)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 onion (chopped fine)
  • 1 bunch green onions (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley (chopped)
  • 1 tbs. paprika
  • 2 white potatoes (quartered)
  • 2 turnips (quartered)
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Boil hen in water until tender.
  2. Reduce stock to 2 quarts.
  3. Add remaining ingredients.
  4. Simmer for 30 minutes, until potatoes are tender.
  5. Season for taste

Deep Fried Okra

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. fresh whole okra
  • 1 cup corn meal
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning

Cooking Instructions

  1. Wash okra.
  2. Slice in 1/2 inch round pieces.
  3. Season well.
  4. Pour corn meal into a brown paper bag.
  5. Add okra and shake well to coat.
  6. Slowly add and fry coated okra in deep fat until brown.
  7. Remove with slotted spoon and drain

Serve as an appetizer or as a vegetable side dish.

Eggplant, Shrimp, & Crab Casserole

Ingredients

  • 1 eggplant
  • 1 large onion (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup celery (chopped)
  • 1 bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup parsley (minced)
  • 1/2 cup saltine crackers (crushed)
  • 1/4 tsp Louisiana red hot sauce
  • 1 lb. cleaned shrimp
  • 1 lb. crab meat
  • 1/2 lb. parmesan cheese
  • 4 strips bacon
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1 egg
  • Juice from 1/2 lemon
  • 3 garlic cloves (minced)
  • Dash of Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning

Cooking Instructions

  1. Peel and slice eggplant.
  2. Soak in Broussard's seasoned water for 1 hour.
  3. Fry bacon until crisp and remove from skillet.
  4. Add onions, celery, bell pepper, and garlic to skillet.
  5. Fry vegetables for 5 minutes.
  6. Add remaining ingredients.
  7. Mix well and pour into a buttered casserole dish.
  8. Top with cheese and bread crumbs.
  9. Bake 40 minutes at 350 degrees.

Creole Fried Rice

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked rice (day old rice is best)
  • 1/4 lb. chopped ham
  • 1/4 cup green onion (chopped)
  • Dash of Louisiana red hot sauce
  • 3 eggs
  • 1/4 cup bell pepper (chopped)
  • 1/4 cup margarine
  • Broussard's Louisiana Cajun Creole Spicy Seasoning (to taste)

Cooking Instructions

  1. Lightly fry ham, onion, and pepper in margarine until onion is tender.
  2. Add rice to skillet.
  3. Heat until warm all through.
  4. Beat eggs.
  5. Pour over rice and stir until well mixed.
  6. Season to taste.
  7. Add hot sauce.
  8. Continue heating 3 - 4 minutes.
